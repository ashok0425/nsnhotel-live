Company Overview

Company Overview

Kwik Computing Limited was established and incorporated in 2000, with the clear aim of providing high quality printing service to Ugandan businesses.
20 Years later, Kwik Computing Ltd has become one of the leading service providers to multinational organizations, NGO’s, Government departments and many individual business entities.

Kwik computing is dedicated to create a timely and tailored design, printing, stationery, and branding experience for the client coupled with a high end quality product. Our product portfolio has expanded from paper printing to textile printing, branding, engraving, packaging, promotional products and art work design and production.
Our facility has expanded since and moved to Plot 15, 7th Street Industrial Area, Kampala and, uses state of the art technologies. We have acquired a multi- talented graphics design and screen printing team to meet the dynamic demands of our clients.

In April 2019, Kwik Computing LTD became a UNBS ISO 9001:2015 Certified Company, Certificate Number UNBS/QMS/0031

Our dedication to quality with perfection has attracted an enviable clientele with our reach expanding from Uganda to Kenya, Burundi, South Sudan, Congo, Mali and, the Central African Republic.
Our belief is to build a strong in house team of well-equipped, competent individuals with the capacity to handle any magnitude of tasks from concept development to project actualization.

We look forward to meeting you to find out how we can best meet you printing, design, branding and stationery needs.

Quality Policy

KWIK is committed to be the leading provider of printing, graphic design, branding, screen printing, embroidery, stationary products and services in Eastern Africa and beyond. This shall be done through employing highly qualified, experienced and well-motivated personnel; using latest modern equipment and latest printing technologies, while implementing a QMS based on ISO 9001:2015; and provide a conducive and safe working environment.

We in KWIK highly regard our customers and at all times we shall strive to satisfy our customers, statutory and regulatory requirements, and even exceed their expectations.
We commit to continually improve our quality management system, and we shall communicate and make all our staff understand and uphold our quality policy at all times.
